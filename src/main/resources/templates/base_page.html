Credit scoring is a system for assessing the creditworthiness (credit risks) of a person based on numerical statistical methods. Credit scoring is widely used both by large banks, microfinance organizations, and in consumer (shop) express lending for small amounts. It is also possible to use it in the business of mobile operators, insurance companies, etc. Scoring consists in assigning points for filling out a certain questionnaire developed by credit risk assessors and underwriters. According to the results of the points scored, the system automatically makes a decision to approve or refuse to issue a loan.
